About Me

I studied Chinese and Japanese in college, worked in various industries and in multiple capacities ranging from accounting to Financial Service Consultant before settling into a career in software sales and development. I previously worked as the Director of Sales Operations and SalesForce CRM for two enterprise software companies where I learned a lot about software sales and even more about SalesForce administration. In my current role, I am the Director of Systems and I am the product owner of our SalesForce implementation. It is by far the most challenging role in my career and I hope that I can parlay what I learn in UCLA's Coding Bootcamp to ensure the success of the project. In my spare time I like to hike, needlepoint, and play video games. I don't watch television but I am a YouTube addict so I have a lot in common with your average 12 year old.
